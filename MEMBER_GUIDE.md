# PSOTS Group Member Guide

## 🤖 Understanding the Moderation Bot

**For All Group Members**

---

## What is the PSOTS Bot?

The **PSOTS Moderation Bot** automatically monitors group messages to maintain a respectful, on-topic community.

**What it does:**
- 📱 Scans every message sent in the group
- 🔍 Checks against community guidelines
- 🗑️ Deletes messages that violate rules
- 📬 Sends you private warnings
- 📊 Tracks violation history

**Goal:** Keep the group focused, respectful, and organized.

## What Gets Deleted?

### ❌ Commercial Messages (Buy/Sell)

**Examples that get deleted:**
- "I want to buy a car, anyone selling?"
- "Selling old furniture, ₹500"
- "Flat for rent - 1BHK, ₹15,000/month"
- "Mobile phone available - contact me"

**Why?** Group is for socializing, not commerce. Use marketplace apps instead.

---

### ❌ Political Content

**Examples that get deleted:**
- "Everyone should vote for BJP"
- "Congress is the best party"
- "What do you think about this election?"
- "Political news discussion"

**Why?** Politics causes conflict. Keep it out of group chats.

---

### ❌ Religious Solicitation

**Examples that get deleted:**
- "Temple donation drive this Sunday"
- "Please contribute to church building"
- "Collect funds for religious cause"
- "Prayer meeting at my place"

**Why?** Respect all religions; no solicitation in group.

---

### ❌ Spam & Promotions

**Examples that get deleted:**
- "AMAZING OFFER! Limited time! Click here!"
- "Earn ₹10,000 working from home!"
- "Refer a friend and earn ₹500!"
- "Check out this business opportunity"

**Why?** Keep group free from unsolicited promotions.

---

### ❌ Abusive Language

**Examples that get deleted:**
- Profanities (Hindi or English)
- Insults to other members
- Personal attacks
- Disrespectful language

**Why?** Maintain respectful community tone.

---

### ✅ What's ALWAYS Allowed

**Great messages:**
- Friendly conversation
- Sharing experiences
- Asking questions
- Supporting others
- Event celebrations (Chhath, Diwali, Holi, Eid, etc.)
- Group announcements from admins
- News and discussions (non-political)

## When You Get a Violation

### 1️⃣ First Violation

**What happens:**
- Your message is deleted
- You get a private Telegram DM

**DM Message:**
```
Hi [Your Name], your message was removed for: 
Buy/Sell Content. Please follow group guidelines. 🙏

View your violations
```

**Your count:** 1 violation

---

### 2️⃣ Second Violation

**What happens:**
- Your message is deleted
- You get another DM (2nd warning)

**DM Message:**
```
Hi [Your Name], this is your 2nd warning for 
Buy/Sell Content. Please be careful. 🙏

View your violations
```

**Your count:** 2 violations

---

### 3️⃣ Third+ Violations

**What happens:**
- Your message is deleted
- You get a warning DM
- **Admin is notified**

**DM Message:**
```
Hi [Your Name], 3rd violation reached. 
Admin notified. 🙏

View your violations & appeal
```

**Your count:** 3+ violations  
**Admin action:** May review your behavior

---

### 🚨 Extreme Violations (5+, 10+)

**At 5 violations:**
- Critical alert to admin
- "Repeat offender" flag
- Serious warning in DM

**At 10 violations:**
- Extreme alert to admin
- Risk of group removal
- Direct admin contact likely

## Appealing a Violation

### If You Think It Was Wrong:

**Steps:**
1. Click link in bot DM
2. Review why it was flagged
3. If it seems unfair:
   - Note the issue
   - Find admin contact on violation page
   - Message them directly

**What to explain:**
- Why the message wasn't a violation
- Context if important
- Acknowledge if you made a mistake

**Admin will consider:**
- ✅ Was it really a violation?
- ✅ Was context misunderstood?
- ✅ Is this your first mistake?
- ✅ Pattern of behavior?

**Timeline:** Admin reviews within 24 hours

## FAQ

**Q: Can I delete a flagged message myself?**  
A: Bot deletes it automatically. You can see it was removed in violation history.

**Q: How long does violation history stay?**  
A: 30 days. After 30 days with no violations, your count resets to 0.

**Q: Can admins delete my message manually?**  
A: Yes, but it's documented in logs. Admins follow same rules as bot.

**Q: What if bot makes a mistake?**  
A: Appeal it! Contact admins through violation page. False positives are reviewed.

**Q: Can I see other people's violations?**  
A: No, only your own. Privacy is protected.

**Q: What's the worst that can happen?**  
A: After 10 violations, you may be removed from group. Admin decides.

**Q: How do I get back if removed?**  
A: Appeal to admin. Show you've learned. Follow rules strictly.

**Q: Does the bot monitor voice messages?**  
A: No, only text messages.
