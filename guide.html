Amenities

🏊 Swimming Pool

⏰Closed every Monday. Open all other days: 6:00 AM – 1:00 PM and 3:00 PM – 8:00 PM. Timings subject to change — check MyGate for updates.

Rules

  • ●Proper swimwear and head caps mandatory. No undergarments, boxers, pants, denims, or T-shirts.
  • ●Shower before entering the pool — always.
  • ●Children under 15 must be accompanied by an adult (18+) at all times.
  • ●Children under 4 years are not allowed in the main pool.
  • ●No food, refreshments, or colored drinks. Only water in plastic/steel bottles allowed.
  • ●No pets in or around the pool area.
  • ●No diving, rough play, acrobatics, or boisterous behaviour.
  • ●No cycling or skateboards in the pool periphery area.
  • ●Persons under influence of alcohol or intoxicants are prohibited.
  • ●Residents with open wounds, infections, or contagious diseases cannot use the pool.
  • ●Training limited to maximum 30% of pool area at any given time.
  • ●Do not use clubhouse amenities or elevators before drying off and changing.
ℹ️Residents use the pool at their own risk. PSOTSAOA and EC are not responsible for accidents or injuries.

Rules

🚗 Vehicles & Parking

Traffic Rules

  • ●Speed limit: 15 km/h — podium and basements both
  • ●Drive clockwise only on concrete roads
  • ●Keep left. No overtaking. No high beams at night.
  • ●PSOTS is a honk-free community
  • ●Pedestrians always have right of way
  • ●No vehicles on pathways, non-concrete paths, or lawns
  • ●No driving lessons — strictly prohibited

Parking Rules

  • ●Park only in your allotted basement parking spot
  • ●No parking in visitor slots (podium level)
  • ●Emergency/Disabled parking — strictly for intended use
  • ●Guest parking free up to 2 days. Apply for visitor pass 3 days in advance for longer stays.
  • ●Guest parking charges: 4W ₹250/day · 2W ₹100/day (beyond 2 days)
  • ●No outside vehicles in basements

Parking Rental (Vacant Slots)

  • ●Individual slot: ₹1,500/month incl. GST · Linked slot: ₹2,500/month
  • ●Minimum 6 months commitment. Apply by emailing: sots.helpdesk@prestigeconstructions.co.in
  • ●Sub-letting of rented slots is strictly prohibited. Auto-renewed for max 3 years total.
⚠️Security can lock vehicles in violation. Vehicles parked unauthorised beyond 5 days reported to local police.
Rules

♻️ Garbage Segregation & Waste

🟢 Wet/Organic Waste

  • Vegetable/fruit peels
  • Cooked food/leftovers
  • Egg shells, bones
  • Tea bags, coffee grinds
  • Fallen leaves, puja flowers
  • Food-soiled tissue

🔵 Dry Waste

  • Plastic/paper covers, bottles
  • Newspapers, cardboard
  • Metal cans, foil containers
  • Unbroken glass bottles
  • Thermocol, rubber, mops
  • Hair, coconut shells

🔴 Sanitary/Reject Waste

  • Diapers, sanitary pads
  • Medicines, bandages
  • Nails, blades, broken glass (wrap in paper)
  • Used syringes
  • Swept dust, used tissues

Collection Timings

  • ●Keep waste outside apartment only between 9:00 AM – 12:00 PM daily.
  • ●Take bins back inside by 12:30 PM after collection.
  • ●Early morning? Use CPCB-certified compostable bags — not before 6:00 AM.
  • ●Wet waste not collected if not segregated or in plastic bags.
  • ●Never put paint, thinner, cement into drains or toilets.
  • ●Never put diapers or sanitary napkins in drains or toilets.
  • ●E-waste (batteries, bulbs, CDs) — hand over separately to housekeeping.
⚠️Garbage kept outside apartment beyond allowed timings: ₹500 fine per instance.

🏠 Move-in & Move-out

Charges

  • ●New owner (purchase): ₹2,000 + GST one-time membership. No move-in/out charge.
  • ●Tenant move-in: ₹1,500 + GST
  • ●Tenant move-out: ₹1,500 + GST
  • ●Blood relation transfers: No membership charge

Key Rules

  • ●Apply via MyGate at least 15 days before move date
  • ●Move-in/out: 9 AM – 6 PM only — not on public holidays
  • ●Service lifts only for luggage, furniture, boxes
  • ●NOC approval takes maximum 7 days
  • ●Get admin NOC + finance NOC before move-out
  • ●Return RFID sticker and Clubhouse ID card on move-out day
⚠️If previous occupant had unpaid dues and moved out without NOC, all dues transfer to the new occupant/owner.

Rules

🐾 Pets

✅Pets can move freely throughout PSOTS. This policy follows Animal Welfare Board of India guidelines and Government of Karnataka notification.

Where Pets Are NOT Allowed

  • ●Inside the Club House
  • ●Swimming pool and pool periphery
  • ●Inside Supermarket and Café
  • ●Skating rink, indoor/outdoor sports areas
  • ●Children's play areas
  • ●Inside Banquet Halls

Registration Required

  • ●Register pet with Property Management Office — email helpdesk with Annexure PP1.
  • ●Provide: breed, age, vaccination date, next vaccination date.
  • ●Keep vaccination records updated.

Conduct Rules

  • ●Always keep pets on leash in common areas.
  • ●Clean up pet waste immediately — dispose in bio-waste bins only (not dry waste bins).
  • ●If pet urinates on pathways — water down the area.
  • ●Passenger lifts: if another resident is uncomfortable, step out and give preference.
  • ●Only adults should handle pets in common areas.
  • ●Do not leave pets alone outside apartments or chain them in common areas.
  • ●Commercial breeding of pets inside PSOTS is prohibited.
  • ●Barking is natural — cannot be used to restrict pets. Make efforts to control during night hours.
⚠️Failure to clean pet waste in common areas: ₹1,000 fine per instance.
Rules

🔼 Elevator Usage

Rules

  • ●Do not press buttons repeatedly or hold elevator doors open with foot.
  • ●Follow overload limits — last person must step out on overload alarm.
  • ●Children under 6 must not travel alone in elevators.
  • ●Service lifts only for goods, luggage, furniture during move-in/out.
  • ●No scratching, writing, or vandalising elevator walls — ₹1,000 fine per incident.
  • ●No littering. No smoking or drinking.
  • ●Always give way to people alighting first.
  • ●In case of fire — do not use elevators.
🆘Stuck in elevator? Hit the alarm button and inform security. Do not panic — there is enough air. Do not exit without checking lift is level with the floor. Emergency contact numbers are displayed inside every elevator.

👥 Visitor Management

Guests

  • ●Guests approved via MyGate app by resident before entry.
  • ●Guests may use parks and kids' play area but not sports amenities or swimming pool.
  • ●Guests park only in designated visitor parking areas.
  • ●Any damage by guests is the resident's responsibility.

Service Staff & Delivery

  • ●All daily service staff must have Property Management Office ID cards.
  • ●Register daily service staff in MyGate app.
  • ●Couriers and delivery need resident approval via MyGate before entry.
  • ●Encourage all service staff to use service lifts.
Rules

⚡ Electric Vehicle Charging

Key Rules

  • ●Installation only by a licensed Class-1 Electrical Contractor.
  • ●Only AC slow charging (230V/15A single phase, max 2.5KW) permitted.
  • ●BESCOM energy meter must be in homeowner's name.
  • ●Installation only in your own registered parking slot — not rented slots.
  • ●Three-phase power connections not permitted.
  • ●Must include: Modular Fire Extinguisher + RCBO protection.
  • ●Sign undertaking on ₹100 stamp paper. Submit BESCOM bill + contractor license.
⚠️Non-compliance penalty: ₹5,000 per month. Residents are fully liable for any damage from their EVCS installation. Existing installations must be compliant within 31 days of notification.
